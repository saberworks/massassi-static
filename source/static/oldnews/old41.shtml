Holy Schlamoley! - Slug
*droooool*.. It's about 1:30 AM, Thursday night (Friday morning I suppose), and I just got outta a game of DFA, Turbo Ritalin Edition (Death From Above's MP pack).. Wow.. Some of the most fun I've had in a game of JK ever.. The weapons are just plain awesome, and it includes 2 guns not available in the SP demo (Well, one of them was available if you cheated).. The doubled-up Tec-9s, and the SLAT (A HUGE rocket launcher).. I of course, tore the game up, with my trusty Shotgun, Amelia (I felt the need to name it mid-way through the game..) The Tec-9s don't seem to be terribly effective, but man are they fun.. I feel like Neo when I run around, 2 guns blazing at any poor sap in front of me.. I could say I love DFA, Turbo Ritalin Edition, but that word is only a pale shadow of the feelings I have for it.. All in all, the absolute second you see this mod posted, GRAB IT! I'll see if I can post some screens tomorrow, gotta check with Ram I suppose.. Wasn't quite sure on how much he wants to keep hidden until release..

This is an alpha v1.01 demo of Thief II: The Metal Age. It works great, movement is a bit hairy though. Instead, think of Thief 2 as a "First Person Sneaker."
