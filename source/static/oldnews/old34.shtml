Spaceport Changes - Slug
Got some great news from Chemelion, over at The Mos Eisley Spaceport. They're changing their rating system to a style that makes way more sense. Here's what Chem had to say.
I have, in the past, put just a score of 1 if the category did not pertain to you. Such as if the level didn't have any cogs, then it got a 1 for cog score, which in the end hurts the final score. So, now, if the category doesn't include you, I take it out, and it makes the score a lot better. If I have the time, I will go back, and even out all the past reviews that have gotten screwed-over by this scoring problem.
My own level got hit by this little oddity, as it had no cogs in it.. Not that I'm bitter or anything.. I haven't been staying up late at night, drawing sketches of Chem's grisly demise, planning, and plotting, and waiting patiently for the day of reckoning.. Seriously, I haven't..

PlanetVampire Launches! - Brian
As you may or may not know, I have long been involved with a vampire site, Vampire Central. However, we recently had the opportunity to join the GameSpy Network. Considering the size of their audience and the benefits they offer, we chose to accept their offer. For the past few weeks, my staff and I have been frantically gathering information and setting up what we feel will be the premiere V:tM site on the internet.

Confused? Vampire: The Masquerade - Redemption is a new game being developed by Nihilistic Software. Most of the members of Nihilistic are former employees of Lucasarts (which is why I decided to get involved in the first place). Vampire is a Role Playing game featuring one of the most advanced 3d engines on the market (developed in-house by a lot of the people who originally created the JK/Sith engine).

A lot of the appeal is based on the fact that Nihilistic is going to release all their editing/development tools to the public as the game itself is launched. These tools, along with support directly from Nihilistic and sites like PlanetVampire, will allow you to create your own Vampire chronicles. If you choose to go deeper, you can create a whole new game!

Well, without further delay, PlanetVampire today is featuring 6 exclusive (and rather amazing) screenshots. Also over there we've posted an interview with Rob Huebner, lead programmer and vice-president over at Nihilistic Software (he was also a senior programmer on Jedi Knight).

There's a lot of info over there, so go check it out! here's the link: PlanetVampire.com.
