Another update on personal/site status. I've received some applications (in response to our "help wanted"), and we're sorting through them, expect the new staff members to be announced soon (probably tomorrow).

As for me, I will be leaving for a month (yep, you read it right, a month). I'm being shipped TDY (temporary duty) to Mississippi for some training, so I won't have access to my computer, and thus, I won't be able to receive email or update the site.

This comes at an interesting time in Massassi history. For one, we turn a year old this Friday! I still can't believe this site took off like it did. Old JK sites have died and/or moved on to non-jk content (jk.net, jk outpost, votj, jedi legacy (well it's still there, but rarely has anything jk on it...), sith temple, more). I will be starting a site dedicated to Star Wars first person type shooters (Jedi Knight/MotS can't really be classed as first person shooters due to the 3rd person view, but whatever). It will be a news site (Massassi can't really shave it's reputation as an editing site, not that I want it to). Massassi will most likely be hosted by this new site, so things around here won't change much at all. When other LEC shooters come out that can be edited (Indiana Jones, another Star Wars shooter, etc), we will start editing sites (similar to Massassi, but hopefully with better names ;-) for each of them. I'm looking forward to covering another game -- I will organize the site better (when Massassi started, I had JUST learned html, and had no idea the site would turn out to be as big as it is).

On another note, I was looking through the old news this morning, and there's some funny stuff there, like when we broke 5000 hits (at the time I thought that was amazing). Help Wanted @ The Massassi Temple - Brian
That's right, we're looking to add a couple of people to our staff. Please, if you're not dedicated to doing daily updates, don't apply. Here are the positions I'm looking for:

  • Level Database: 1 person to handle posting all levels that come into the Temple. We handle our levels as follows: An email comes in with the attached level (and sometimes some screenshots). You will have to upload the level to our ftp site, then fill out a form using the information in the email. If this information is incorrect/missing, you will have to look in the readme, or email the author to get it. If there are screenshots, they will have to be uploaded and added to the form. This position does not take up much time on a daily basis, but I do expect that each level is posted within 2 days of being recieved. More information available on request.
  • Newsmaster: This person will be updating news on a regular basis. This doesn't mean waiting for an email to come in and posting it at your leisure, this means going out daily to the other JK / Star Wars / Other 3d Gaming Sites and looking for news that applies to JK and/or JK editing. We will also be covering the next editable Star Wars first person shooter when/if it comes out. We update via a very easy to use update script, however, knowledge of HTML is desirable. Depending on the qualifications/experience/quality of the applications, I may be choosing two people for this position. Our goal here is daily updates of JK news - be it editing or not.
  • Message Boards Administrator: This person will be in charge of moderating and archiving the message boards. HTML experience is a must, unix/cgi/perl experience is desirable. If you can set up/modify a wwwboard, that will suffice. This person doesn't have to do daily updates of the site, but they will be cleaning up/archiving the message boards whenever it needs to be done.

When sending your applications, please include a detailed description of your knowledge of JK/HTML/CGI/ETC. Please also include past experiences with other sites (JK related or not).

As a note: I will not bring on someone that has terrible grammar/spelling. It's not that I have something against this, it's just not what we want on our site. Please, make sure your application tells a lot about you, your experience, knowledge, etc. Please do not apply if you aren't dedicated to daily updates (of course a missed day here and there is no big deal...).
