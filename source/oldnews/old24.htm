Beta Testing! - Slug
To start off, this message is in no way aimed at anyone in particular.. It appears after posting an update to a level, but is in no way aimed at that author - God knows he's not the first one to send in a revised level.. People - PLEASE, PLEASE, PLEASE BETA TEST. Browse through our news archives if you want to see how many updates we've had to make, all of them were because the person failed to beta test.. They have always been blaringly obvious errors that would've shown up in the first beta test.. Just please, take pride in your work people.. Test thoroughly, don't release levels because you're bored.. You not only release levels that nobody wants to play (and in some cases, because of glaring errors, *can't* play), but you just make extra work for webmasters of the sites you submit the file to.. Believe it or not, I don't jump out of bed in the morning and say "Gee, I hope someone sent a revised file in today!" If you don't have friends that you can test your level with in multiplayer, then use Slade's beta testing group (http://www.fforce.net/betatest)..
