SavageX Updates - Brian
SavageX's Command Center has been updated with a ton of info. In addition to a list of a bunch of new weapons, skins, items, and features, he's got a blurb about a new skill system he's working on.
I am also working on a "Warrior Experience Skill" System that replaces the forcepowers. These, however, are not powers at all. They are stats you can add to the player. You don't bind them to hotkeys because they modify the player's fighting ability so don't think they are force powers. There are 12 stats in all and are divided into three sections.

The first four skills are the Basic Skills in which a player can increase his Strength (adds to melee weapons power), Increase his Vitality (adds more Health units), incease his agility (makes him move faster), and the last one is Endurance (how much damage the player takes.)
For the rest, please visit SavageX's Command Center.
