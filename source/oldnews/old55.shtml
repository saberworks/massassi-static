Not so much JK stuff going on, but the world of Star Wars gaming seems to be hopping with all the Obi-Wan and SWO information. GC: EverQuest is a big hit. How does that help you make a Star Wars game?

Life Beyond The Phantom Menace - Kedri
With all the fanfare of the video release of Episode 1, another piece of Star Wars merchandise was released on Tuesday that got overlooked. It's Star Wars: The Essential Chronology, a book that spans the thousands of years of the documented Star Wars history. It's a great reference for any kind of Star Wars fan, from the casual SW novel reader who wants to put things into perspective, to the die-hard fanatic who thirsts for every detail they can find. This book's got it all.

Unlike the debatable quality of The Phantom Menace (if all the threads on our Discussion Forum are any indication), this book will not disappoint.
