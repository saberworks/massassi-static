I'm very pleased so far with the results of the MPG! We will be adding new required files every weekend, so keep a look out! I will be working on some enhancements to the system in the near future.

On to other things...

As you have probably noticed, our server has been a bit slow lately. On top of that, about 1/3 of the people I surveyed often can't get to our subdomains (http://something.massassi.net). Our tech support guys have been looking into this problem, but no resolution has yet been found. I suspect its server load (during peak times, we overload our memory), but I can't be sure.

Anyway, this weekend I'm going to purchase a computer and install Linux on it. I'm going to learn all the software necessary to administer a killer web server - and as soon as that's finished, The Massassi Temple will be purchasing a killer new box to serve our network. I'm estimating that the new server will be up and running in about two months.

Does that mean you have to deal with these problems for two months!? No! I am working with UGO to take the forums off this server, which will basically leave this bad-boy w/out a load (the forums use the most CPU time and RAM). I am also working to get some more RAM added to the server. No exact dates on the above two items, but I am working as fast as I can.

Jedi Knight Pong - blujay
I just uploaded LikWid's JK Pong and I just have to mention it. I was his main beta tester and I must say it's one of the most creative and most fun JK mods there is. It's all in one GOB that goes in your Episode directory, so no worrying about running JK with a batch file or Patch Commander. It has a scoring system that adds the difference between your and your opponent's score to your score in JK after the match so that you can have little tournaments by playing several matches, and after so many matches the player with the most points wins the tournament. And it's simply a lot of fun. Each of the five levels requires a certain technique. And included is a "for authors" file that explains how to make your own Pong levels. I strongly encourage you to download it and try it out with a friend. You can play for hours. And I hope that people will start making their own Pong levels too. Ok, I'm stepping off my rant box now.  (c;
