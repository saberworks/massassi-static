Jedi Knight Screensaver Project - blujay
Mike Starr sent this update on the project:
With only two short weeks to go, the Jedi Knight Screensaver Project is drawing to a close. We have a pretty good variety of pics for the program, but we can always use more. Folks, there's no such thing as a bad pic. Give it a shot and send it in!

In case you haven't seen the previous posts, this is a project to make a screensaver from screenshots taken in Jedi Knight and Mysteries of the Sith. I accept any and all pics from the basic stock levels up to completely user-made environments. PCX files are fine, JPG/JPEG and BMP files are preferred and putting it all in a ZIP file would be nice. And, by all means, multiple submissions are fine.

While you're at it, try your skill as a MIDI composer and make a Star Wars medley to go along with the project. I will take the best written MIDI with the longest run time and/or themes incorporated. Act now, before the competition heats up!

Try your hand as a Jedi-photographer and/or Star Wars musician! Send your submissions to JKScreensaver@hotmail.com!

On a side note, I do not have the e-mail address for the artist named JK[Meteor]. The address will be needed later, so please contact me at orionn@md.freei.net as soon as possible.
